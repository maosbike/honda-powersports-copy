E-Clutch

Scroll

Honda's E-Clutch technology eliminates stalling by automating clutch control, making motorcycle riding more accessible. Enjoy smooth, effortless gear changes without the need for manual clutch operation, reducing the learning curve for new riders and enhancing convenience for experienced ones. Whether on city streets or carving canyons, E-Clutch offers a seamless and worry-free ride.

Next level riding

Honda's E-Clutch automates clutch control, eliminating stalling and ensuring smooth, precise gear changes. This innovation makes riding easier and more enjoyable for everyone.

Automatic clutch control

Automates the clutch, eliminating stalling and making riding accessible to beginners while enhancing convenience for seasoned riders.

Smooth-shifting

Ensures seamless and precise gear transitions, providing a more comfortable and enjoyable ride across various terrains.

Lower learning curve

Reduces the complexity of manual clutch operation, making it easier for new riders to start and enjoy motorcycle riding with confidence.
