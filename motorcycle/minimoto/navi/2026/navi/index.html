Simple to ride. Hard to resist.

Twist-and-go, let’s roll

109cc single-cylinder engine

One of the best things about this bike is its reliable Honda engine. It's air-cooled for simplicity, offers great fuel efficiency, and makes plenty of power while also being low-maintenance and super dependable.

Twist-and-go, let’s roll

Automatic transmission

The continuous variable transmission (CVT) requires no clutch or shifting. Simply start the vehicle, twist the throttle, and go. There is also no neutral or park mode.

Twist-and-go, let’s roll

Electric starter

Starting the engine is a breeze with both an electric starter and a backup kickstarter.

Twist-and-go, let’s roll

Low seat height

A low, 30-inch seat height allows for easy mounting and flat-footing. And the long, flat seat makes finding the right riding position easy.

Lightweight and nimble

Accessorized Model Shown

Four great colors

The four excellent colors available for 2026 are: White, Arctic Silver Metallic, Blue Metallic and Pearl Red.

Standout style

Its distinctive urban style will make you stand out wherever you go. And it's tailor-made for personalization.

Telescopic-fork suspension

Like a full-sized motorcycle, it features telescopic fork front suspension, providing excellent control and road feedback.

Honda Eco Tech

The Honda Eco Tech (HET) engine design offers the power and convenience of a traditional internal combustion engine while being environmentally conscious.

Strong steel frame

The underbone steel frame is built to handle rough city streets and enhance the bike's maneuverability.

Front and rear brakes

Its brakes are set up like those on a full-sized motorcycle. There's a front brake lever on the right-hand handlebar and a rear brake pedal. Its combined braking system makes stops quicker and more secure.

Integrated storage area

If you've got a few things to bring along for the ride, this miniMOTO has you covered. It features a spacious, lockable, built-in storage compartment that fits a laptop, lunch, and more.

Two-up capability

Sharing the fun with a friend is easy thanks to the standard passenger footpegs, two-person seat, and grab rails.

Accessorized Model Shown

Accessories

Honda offers accessories to personalize your new Navi, including a windscreen for added comfort and a rear rack for increased storage.
