Africa Twin

Wherever you’re headed, Honda’s Africa Twin has got the power, poise, and control to take you there – paved road optional.

Build yours Africa Twin

Ready when you are

1084cc twin-cylinder engine

Our narrow 1084cc Unicam® parallel-twin engine delivers torque smoothly throughout the rpm range, making it great for everything from off-road riding to all-day highway cruising.

Ready when you are

Tires and wheels

All Africa Twin models have wire-spoked wheels. The standard models feature 21-inch front and 18-inch rear wheels designed for better off-road performance, while the Adventure Sports ES models get a more road-focused 19-inch front wheel and wider rims.

Ready when you are

Low center of gravity

By placing heavy components like the battery near the bike's center of gravity and using a compact engine, we've created a platform for excellent handling.

Ready when you are

Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™

Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™ are standard across all models, connecting your mobile device to provide access to texts, navigation, and more. Wireless Bluetooth® connection is also available for audio use with optional headsets.

Original and still the best

View all Features

Ride modes

Both models offer six selectable riding modes: Tour, Urban, Gravel, Off-Road, User 1, and User 2—all managed through the 6.5-inch touchscreen TFT display. The last two can be customized to your individual preferences.

Selectable ABS

Both models feature a three-mode Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS), allowing you to choose between On-Road, Off-Road, or a rear-ABS delete mode for enhanced performance during more aggressive dirt riding.

TFT touchscreen display

A 6.5-inch TFT touchscreen instrument panel offers glove-friendly controls with three displays, allowing you to view a variety of information based on your preferences.
