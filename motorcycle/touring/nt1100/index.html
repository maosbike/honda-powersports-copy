Six-speed automatic Dual-Clutch Transmission

Experience the future of riding with our revolutionary automatic DCT gearbox, merging durability and performance seamlessly. Featuring robust steel gears for exceptional strength and direct power transfer, it provides the versatility to choose between fully automatic shifting or engaging manual control with intuitive paddle shifters.

Born for the commute

1084cc twin-cylinder engine

The 1084cc liquid-cooled Unicam® parallel-twin engine offers an excellent powerband for sport touring, delivering usable torque from idle to redline. It’s great for everything from tight city streets to sweeping backroads to high-speed highways.

Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™

Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™ are now standard on the NT1100, allowing you to connect your mobile device to provide access to texts, navigation, and more. Wireless Bluetooth® connection is also available for audio use with optional headsets.

Five-position adjustable windscreen

Cruise control

With its exceptional comfort and silky-smooth engine, this bike is ideal for long journeys. That’s why we’ve integrated a sophisticated throttle-by-wire cruise control system, ensuring effortless riding on extended trips.

Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC)

Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC) lets you tailor the power delivery to suit varying conditions and skill levels. With three distinct levels, you can adjust the amount of torque and rear-wheel slip for optimal performance on all roads in any weather.

Ride modes

Choose from five riding modes: Tour, Urban, Rain, and two customizable options. Easily adjust power delivery, engine braking, and ABS intervention to suit your preferences, with seamless mode switching for any conditions.
