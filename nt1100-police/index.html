NT1100 Police Motorcycle

Comfortable, cost-effective and offering a level of reliability and performance that have made Honda famous, our NT1100 Police is the smart choice for modern law-enforcement agencies. Powerful, light and technologically advanced, it’s available to qualified departments here in America. Put your officers on the best—the NT1100 Police.

Modern departments need modern solutions

You may not have seen many Honda police bikes on the road, but that’s all about to change. The new NT1100 Police is built for the way agencies use their motorcycles today. Plus it’s lighter and more maneuverable than many of the machines your officers have been using, but still offers all the performance they want and need.

Engineered for the line of duty

Unicam® engine design

Honda’s Unicam engine design, with its compact cylinder-head layout, allows for optimal engine placement and superior performance in terms of both handling-related weight distribution and airflow-related horsepower. Plus, the design is light and narrow.

Cornering ABS

The integrated six-axis IMU is a standard feature of the NT1100 Police bike's Anti-lock Braking System (ABS). It automatically adds the correct level of anti-lock based on the degree the bike and rider are leaned over, along with vehicle speed and road surface.

Light and maneuverable

Because the NT1100 Police is a thoroughly modern design, it’s far lighter than the other law-enforcement motorcycle offerings most departments are using. That makes it more maneuverable and less fatiguing to ride, especially for parade and escort use, as well as in urban operations.

Built to serve

1084cc twin-cylinder engine

The NT1100 Police offers the best engine choice for a law-enforcement agency. The parallel-twin design is narrow, and delivers torque everywhere from idle to redline.

Honda Selectable Torque Control

With Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC) you can dial in exactly the kind of power delivery you want for the conditions at hand, from parade and escort use to pursuits.

Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™

Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™ are standard on the NT1100 Police. They seamlessly integrate with mobile devices, allowing easy access to calls, texts, maps, and more.

Selectable riding modes

The NT1100 Police offers five selectable riding modes: Urban, Rain, Tour, and two user-programmable settings.

Biaxial counterbalancer

Using counterbalancing elements on two axes cancel out both primary and coupling vibrations from the engine. The result: solid engine mounting for superior strength and handling along with an extraordinarily smooth, vibration-free ride.

Cruise control

Our throttle-by-wire technology lets us offer cruise control, which can be a big help on long transits or when your officers need to ride at a steady pace for longer distances.

TFT touchscreen display

The NT1100 Police features a 6.5-inch touch-panel LCD multi-information dash display. You can choose between multiple display options depending on how much information you want to see at once.

Custom equipped for your department’s needs

An extensive line of law-enforcement accessories have been developed specifically for the NT1100 Police: radio and siren mounts, lighting packages, and much more—in short, everything your department needs. Your local dealer can install them, or you can have them installed at a special Honda-authorized police center.
