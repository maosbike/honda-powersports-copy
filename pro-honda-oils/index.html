Performance in action

Pro Honda Oils and Chemicals are put through the most rigorous performance and durability tests imaginable, measured against professional racing and standards.  From the high banks of Daytona to the unforgiving Baja desert, Team Honda’s racing motorcycles and ATVs take these products to the limit and beyond.  Some oils and chemicals are based on automotive products, but Pro Honda Oils and Chemicals are purpose-built for the demands of intense Powersports racing. 

How Pro Honda Oils are created

A request for new oil

All Pro Honda Powersports Oils are born from a request by Honda Powersports R&D to their companion office at Idemitsu Lubricants. 

Formulated to exacting specifications

Idemitsu begins each new product development with an ultrahigh-quality base oil. Engineers adjust this base with anti-foam and anti-wear agents, corrosion inhibitors, friction modifiers, and anything else needed to maximize the oil’s properties for use with a particular engine. 

Extensive lab testing

The new formula undergoes extensive clinical trials. These trials inform further refinement as the formula is adjusted, improved, and tested again until it’s deemed suitable for field tests. 

Comprehensive Honda bench and field testing

Honda Powersports R&D now subjects the prototype formula to comprehensive field testing in the appropriate motorcycle, ATV, SxS, or scooter.
